SFDLE

An online trivia game for fighting game fans.

Story

Fighting games have always been more than just a hobby to me; they’re a passion. From pulling off intricate combos to mastering the psychology of a match, the experience is both thrilling and rewarding. Yet, I often find myself longing for a way to celebrate this genre in a fun, engaging way — something like a daily challenge that sharpens your knowledge and skills while celebrating the rich history of fighting games.

This is why I dream of creating a Fighting Game DLE. Imagine a platform where players guess iconic moves, identify legendary characters, or test their knowledge of frame data — a daily puzzle that connects the fighting game community like never before.

It’s the resource I’ve always wanted, and the project I’m determined to bring to life.

Features

Login to Access Achievements

Create an account and log in to access your personal achievements. Your progress, high scores, and milestones will be tracked, allowing you to compete with others and strive for new goals. The more you play, the more you accomplish!

Achievements

Unlock a variety of achievements as you progress through the game. From mastering combos to completing specific challenges, each achievement adds to your personal journey and showcases your skill and dedication.

Leaderboard

Climb the leaderboard and see how you rank against other players. Compete for the top spots by achieving high scores, completing challenges, and showing off your skills. The leaderboard motivates you to push yourself to become the best in the community!

About Us

I’m Johan Rueda, a junior developer who completed the "Fondamental" program at Holberton School. This project was created to fulfill the requirements of my curriculum and turned out to be an incredible experience. It allowed me to apply my skills in a practical setting while learning new techniques in web development and system programming. The project not only enhanced my technical abilities but also gave me valuable insights into real-world problem-solving. This milestone has been an important part of my journey, and I’m excited to continue learning and growing as a developer.
